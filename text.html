Text Message Scams

What are text message scams?

Email scams are fraudulent emails that aim to deceive people into sharing sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, or bank details. Scammers often pose as legitimate companies or individuals to gain trust and exploit victims for financial or personal gain.

Features you are likely to find in text messages that are scams :

Scam emails often use urgent threats to prompt quick action, like claiming your account will be suspended. They may include shady links and impersonate trusted organizations with fake sender details.

What should you do if you identify a text message scam?

If you identify an email scam, report it to your email provider and mark it as spam or phishing. Then delete the message and avoid clicking any links or replying to the sender.
