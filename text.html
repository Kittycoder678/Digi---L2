Text Message Scams

What are text message scams?

Text message scams are fake messages that trick people into giving personal info, clicking harmful links, or sending money. Scammers often pretend to be trusted groups like banks or delivery services.

Features you are likely to find in text messages that are scams :

Scam texts often use urgent language and ask for personal info or money. They may include strange links or come from unknown numbers.

What should you do if you identify a text message scam?

If you spot a scam text, don’t click any links or reply. Report it to your mobile provider and delete the message.
