Email Scams

What are Email scams?

Email scams are fake messages that trick people into sharing sensitive info like passwords or bank details. Scammers often pretend to be trusted companies or individuals.

Image licenced under pixabay
Features you are likely to find in emails that are scams :

Scam emails often use urgent threats to prompt quick action, like claiming your account will be suspended. They may include shady links and impersonate trusted organizations with fake sender details.

Image licenced under pixabay
What should you do if you identify a email scam?

If you identify an email scam, report it to your email provider and mark it as spam or phishing. Then delete the message and avoid clicking any links or replying to the sender.
